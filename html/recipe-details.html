Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add milk, eggs, and oil. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving.

Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, grated or finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup vermicelli or thin noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil and sauté the onions until soft and golden.
  2. Add garlic, turmeric, and carrots. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and cherry tomatoes.

Qabuli Palaw

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb (with bone, preferably shank)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Drain before cooking.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the lamb and brown it on all sides. Season with salt, pepper, and spices.
  4. Cover and steam on low heat for 20–30 minutes.
  5. Gently mix before serving. Serve hot with lemon slices or yogurt.

Afghan Kofta (Meatball Stew)

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander or parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1–2 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground meat, grated onion, garlic, egg, herbs, and spices.
  2. Form the mixture into medium-sized meatballs.
  3. Heat oil in a large pot and lightly fry the meatballs just to brown the outside. Set them aside.
  4. Adjust salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot with rice or bread.
